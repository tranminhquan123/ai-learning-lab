# Finance Roadmap

Purpose: learn finance from reliable sources.

## Phase 1: Financial Literacy

- Income and expense basics
- Saving and budgeting
- Compound interest
- Risk and return

## Phase 2: Accounting Basics

- Balance sheet
- Income statement
- Cash flow statement
- Revenue, profit, assets, liabilities, equity

## Phase 3: Business Analysis

- Business model
- Industry structure
- Competitive advantage
- Management quality

## Phase 4: Practice

- Read one company report
- Summarize one business
- Build a simple finance tracker
- Write a short analysis note

## Rule

This roadmap is for education and analysis.
