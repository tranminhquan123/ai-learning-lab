# DaVinci Resolve Roadmap

Purpose: learn practical video editing workflow.

## Phase 1: Basic Workflow

- Import media
- Organize clips
- Cut and trim
- Add text
- Export video

## Phase 2: Audio

- Clean audio
- Adjust volume
- Add music
- Basic mixing

## Phase 3: Visuals

- Transitions
- Simple effects
- Color correction
- Captions

## Phase 4: Practice Projects

- Short product video
- Delivery video
- Social media reel
- Simple talking-head edit

## Weekly Output

Each week should produce one short edited video or one editing exercise.
