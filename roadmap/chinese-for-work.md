# Chinese for Work Roadmap

Purpose: learn Chinese for work and communication.

## Phase 1: Foundation

- Pinyin
- Pronunciation
- Greetings
- Numbers and dates
- Basic sentence patterns

## Phase 2: Work Vocabulary

- Products
- Prices
- Orders
- Delivery
- Customer messages

## Phase 3: Practice

- Ask questions
- Confirm information
- Explain problems
- Write short replies

## Weekly Output

- 20 words
- 10 sentences
- 1 short conversation
